Beaches - Where the Sun Meets the Sand

Popular Beaches

Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep is a group of islands in the Arabian Sea known for their coral reefs, lagoons, and laid-back atmosphere. Bangaram Beach is a popular destination for those seeking a secluded getaway.

Goa

Goa is famous for its beautiful beaches, laid-back atmosphere, and vibrant nightlife. Some of the most popular beaches in Goa include Calangute Beach, Candolim Beach, Baga Beach, and Anjuna Beach.

Karnataka

Karnataka has a long coastline with beaches that cater to different tastes. Gokarna is a popular destination for backpackers, while Om Beach and Kudle Beach are known for their beautiful rock formations.

Popular Destinations

kolkata

Kolkata, also formerly known as Calcutta, is the capital of the Indian state of West Bengal. It's a city rich in history and culture, having been founded as an East India Company trading post in the 17th century. It was India's capital under British rule from 1773 to 1911. Kolkata is also known's as City of Joy . This name comes from a novel by Dominique Lapierre and has stuck ever since.

Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is the capital of the Indian state of Rajasthan. It is a popular tourist destination, known for its forts, palaces, and bazaars. The city was founded in 1727 by Sawai Jai Singh II, the Kachhwaha Rajput ruler of Amer, and was named after him. Jaipur is one of the earliest planned cities of modern India, designed by Vidyadhar Bhattachary

Bangalore

Bangalore, also officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital of the southern Indian state of Karnataka. It's a megacity with a population of over 10 million, making it the third-most populous city and fifth-most populous urban agglomeration in India. Bangalore is also known's as Silicon Valley of India.
